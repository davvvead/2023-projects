Mr. Subin Man

MANAGER

Sarkodie A. Joseph Tuffour, a dedicated male politician hailing from Banko in the Ashanti Region, has a rich history of service and leadership. Born on November 15, 1932, he brings a wealth of experience to his role as a member of the National Democratic Congress (NDC). Tuffour's educational background includes a Teachers' Training Certificate (Certificate B), reflecting his commitment to education and knowledge. He previously worked as a General Contractor before entering the political arena. Serving as a Member of Parliament from 1993 to 1997 in the 1st Parliament, he represented the Subin constituency within the Kumasi Metropolitan District, which was later part of the Ashanti Region. Tuffour has a solid voter base, as evidenced by the 6463 votes he secured, equivalent to 8.89% support. With a strong parliamentary majority, he continues to advocate for the interests of his constituency and the NDC in the vibrant political landscape of Ghana, upholding his Christian faith throughout his career.
